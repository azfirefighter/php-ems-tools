PHP EMS Tools Schedule Help

Click on a time slot (one of the gray boxes in a day) to sign on, edit a signon, or remove a signon, for that day. You may need an administrator to change shifts in the past.

Administrators can click on the box where the date is to insert, edit, or remove a special message for that day, specify a non-default number of crews, or specify another organization providing coverage.

Click on the "<< Month" link at the top to go to the previous month. Click on the "Month >>" link to go to the next month.

When viewing the Day schedule, click on the "Nights" link at the top to switch to the night schedule for that month. When viewing the Night schedule, click on the "Days" link to go to the day schedule.

Click on the "Count Hours" link to view the monthly/yearly hours totals.

Click on the "Current Shift" link to go back to the current shift (day/night) for the current month.

Members shown in blue or with a (P) after their name are probationary members.
